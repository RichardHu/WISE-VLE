Volcanoes and volcanic activity are responsible for some very interesting landforms. In this activity, you will go to a map of the United States and investigate how many active volcanoes there are near where you live and on the opposite coast. By active, we mean volcanoes that have erupted within the last 200 years.

Once you jump to the link below, zoom in by clicking on the area where you live. The red triangles on the map represent the location of active volcanoes.

  1. Count how many volcanoes there are on the West Coast.
  2. Count how many volcanoes there are on the East Coast.
