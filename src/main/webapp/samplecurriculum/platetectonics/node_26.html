How do volcanoes and mountains form? In this project, you will learn about how the many different landforms and geologic events we see and experience on our planet are the result of invisible processes deep within the Earth.


Before beginning the project, we'll take a moment to fill out a questionnaire in the first few steps.


Your responses will not be graded, but they will help your teacher understand what you already know. So don't worry if you don't know the answers—just try your best!
