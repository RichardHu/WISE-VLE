Continental Drift?


Image courtesy of Anglais



Have you ever noticed that the continents South America and Africa look like they fit together? In 1910, a young German scientist named Alfred Wegener attempted to solve the jigsaw puzzle of the continents. Wegener hypothesized that all the continents were once joined together in a single landmass which he called Pangaea. Over time, the continents drifted apart.
