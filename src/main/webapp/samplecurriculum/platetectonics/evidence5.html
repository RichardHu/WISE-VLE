Plate Boundaries and Geologic Features

Tectonic Plates of the World

Now that you've explored the relationship between plate boundaries and continents, let's explore the relationship between plate boundaries and geologic features and events.


Some things to notice on the map include:

  • The oceans are not shown,
  • The blue lines represent plate boundaries,
  • The red triangles represent volcanoes, and
  • The yellow dots represent earthquakes.


You should think about how plate boundaries are important in describing the differences between the geology on your coast and on the opposite coast.
