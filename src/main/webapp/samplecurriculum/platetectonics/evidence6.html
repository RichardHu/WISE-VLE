Types of Plate Boundaries

Image courtesy of NASA

There are three types of plate boundaries on Earth's surface.

  1. Transform boundaries, where two plates slide past each other,
  2. Divergent boundaries, where two plates move away from each other, and
  3. Convergent boundaries, where two plates are moving towards each other.

Each type of plate boundary can give rise to different geological features and events, depending on the types of plates involved.
