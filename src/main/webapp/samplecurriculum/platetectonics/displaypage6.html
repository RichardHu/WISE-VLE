Pose a Question

In this activity, you will think of some questions that you have about the differences in East and West Coast geology. Discuss with your partner questions about earthquakes, volcanoes, or mountain formation. Write two of your own questions OR select two of the following questions to investigate in this project.


Once you have decided on at least TWO questions, record them in the following note.


Possible Questions to Investigate

  • Why are there many more mountains on the West Coast than on the East Coast?
  • Why are there volcanoes on the West Coast and none on the East Coast?
  • What causes a volcano to form in a particular area?
  • Why do earthquakes occur on the West Coast?
