Currents in the Mantle

Image courtesy of BBC

What might be causing plates to move? Scientists are currently investigating the underlying forces causing plate movement, but scientists know that one force involved in plate movement is convection in the mantle.


The mantle is mostly solid, but also partially molten (melted) because of the steady heat and forces applied to the mantle over very long periods of time. It is believed that the mantle is moving very slowly in a circular pattern called convection currents. Convection, like conduction, is a type of heat (thermal energy) transfer.
